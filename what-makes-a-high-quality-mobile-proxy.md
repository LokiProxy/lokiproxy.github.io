## Authenticity of Network Attributes


The primary characteristic of a high-quality mobile proxy is that its IP addresses originate from genuine mobile networks. A genuine mobile network means that IP addresses are assigned by telecom operators to actual smartphones or mobile devices in active use, rather than being virtually generated from data centers through virtualization technologies.


For application scenarios that require verification from a mobile perspective, authentic mobile networks are a prerequisite for ensuring valid verification results.
