## What Is a [Mobile Proxy](https://www.lokiproxy.com/?utm_t=1&utm_i=52)?


A mobile proxy is a proxy service based on real mobile carrier networks. Unlike data center proxies, mobile proxy IPs do not originate from server racks, they are legitimate IPs assigned by carriers to real mobile devices (such as smartphones or tablets) when they connect to cellular networks.


From a technical standpoint, when a user request is forwarded through a proxy server, the target website always receives a legitimate IP assigned by the mobile carrier to a real device, rather than a virtual address from a data center or cloud server. This fundamental difference gives mobile proxies a natural advantage in business scenarios closely tied to mobile traffic.


## Key Considerations When Purchasing a Mobile Proxy


When evaluating mobile proxy services, price should not be the only factor. A comprehensive assessment across multiple dimensions is essential:

### 1. IP Source Authenticity
A quality mobile proxy should originate from legitimate carrier base stations and be emitted by real mobile devices, rather than being disguised data center or virtual equipment. This is the foundational value of a mobile proxy.

### 2. Coverage and Targeting Capabilities
Verify whether the provider covers your target regions based on your business needs. Mobile network environments vary significantly across markets—broader coverage means stronger business adaptability.

### 3. Performance and Stability
Network response latency and access success rates directly impact task efficiency. When selecting a provider, look for publicly available performance data.

### 4. Pricing and Billing Models
Evaluate whether the provider's pricing is reasonable and package thresholds are accessible, based on your budget and business scale. Also consider whether new user trial packages are available for low-cost initial testing.
